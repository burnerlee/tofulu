# Design System - Testino Landing Page

## Color Palette (from Official TOEFL Website)
Based on ETS TOEFL TestReady design:

**Primary Colors:**
- **Background**: White (#FFFFFF) and Light Gray (#F5F5F5)
- **Primary Text**: Teal (#086A6F) for headings and brand elements
- **Body Text**: Black (#000000) or Dark Gray (#333333)
- **Logo Accent**: Teal (#086A6F)
- **Links**: Teal (#086A6F)

**Accent Colors (for CTAs):**
- **Primary CTA**: Teal (#086A6F)
- **CTA Hover**: Darker Teal (#065559)

## Typography
**Font Family:**
- **Primary Fonts**: 'Oswald' and 'Inter' (from Google Fonts)
- **Oswald**: Used for headings, logos, and display text (condensed sans-serif)
- **Inter**: Used for body text, navigation, buttons, and general content (modern sans-serif)
- Fallback: System fonts stack: `-apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Segoe UI', 'Roboto', 'Oxygen', 'Ubuntu', 'Cantarell', 'Fira Sans', 'Droid Sans', 'Helvetica Neue', sans-serif`

**Font Sizes:**
- Hero Heading: 48px - 64px (3rem - 4rem), bold
- Sub-heading: 20px - 24px (1.25rem - 1.5rem), regular
- Body Text: 16px (1rem), regular
- CTA Button: 18px - 20px (1.125rem - 1.25rem), medium weight

**Font Weights:**
- Headings: 700 (bold)
- Body: 400 (regular)
- CTA: 500-600 (medium to semi-bold)

## Layout Principles
1. **Minimalist Design**: Lots of white space, uncluttered
2. **Centered Content**: Hero section content centered horizontally and vertically
3. **Simple Navigation**: Minimal header with logo and essential links
4. **Clear Hierarchy**: Large headline, supporting text, prominent CTA button
5. **Responsive**: Mobile-first approach

## Component Specifications

### Header
- Height: 64px - 80px
- Background: White or transparent
- Logo: Left-aligned
- Navigation: Right-aligned (minimal links)

### Hero Section
- Full viewport height (100vh) or substantial height
- Centered content (flexbox: center both axes)
- Background: Light gray (#F5F5F5) or white
- Padding: Generous (40px - 60px)

### CTA Button
- Background: Teal/Dark Green (#20B2AA or similar)
- Text: White
- Padding: 16px 32px (vertical horizontal)
- Border radius: 8px - 12px
- Font size: 18px - 20px
- Hover: Slight darkening or scale effect
- Shadow: Subtle (optional): 0 2px 8px rgba(0,0,0,0.1)

### Spacing
- Section padding: 60px - 80px vertical
- Element spacing: 24px - 32px between major elements
- Button spacing: 16px - 24px from text above
