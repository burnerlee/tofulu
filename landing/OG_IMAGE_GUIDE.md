# Open Graph Image (og-image.png) Design Guide

## Technical Specifications

### Dimensions
- **Recommended Size**: 1200 x 630 pixels (2:1 aspect ratio)
- **Minimum Size**: 600 x 315 pixels
- **Maximum Size**: 1200 x 630 pixels (optimal)
- **File Format**: JPG or PNG
- **File Size**: Keep under 1MB for fast loading (aim for 200-500KB)

### Why These Dimensions?
- Facebook: 1200 x 630px (recommended)
- Twitter: 1200 x 675px (but accepts 1200 x 630px)
- LinkedIn: 1200 x 627px (but accepts 1200 x 630px)
- 1200 x 630px works well across all platforms

  - "Free Practice Tests • AI Grading • Authentic Experience"

### 4. **Visual Element** (Optional but Recommended)

### 5. **Background**
- **Primary**: Light Gray (#F5F5F5) or White (#FFFFFF) - matching your design system
- **Alternative**: Subtle gradient from Light Gray to White
- Ensure good contrast with text

## Design Best Practices

### ✅ Do's
- **Keep it simple**: Less is more for social media
- **High contrast**: Ensure text is readable on background
- **Brand consistency**: Use your exact brand colors (#086A6F)
- **Readable fonts**: Use large, clear fonts (minimum 24px for body text)
- **Safe zone**: Keep important content 40px from edges
- **Test on mobile**: Preview how it looks on small screens

### ❌ Don'ts
- **Don't overcrowd**: Too much text becomes unreadable
- **Don't use small fonts**: Minimum 24px for body, 60px+ for headlines
- **Don't ignore brand colors**: Stick to your design system
- **Don't use low-res images**: Use high-quality graphics
- **Don't put important info at edges**: Social platforms may crop edges

## Testing Your Image

### Before Publishing
1. **Preview on Facebook**: Use [Facebook Sharing Debugger](https://developers.facebook.com/tools/debug/)
2. **Preview on Twitter**: Use [Twitter Card Validator](https://cards-dev.twitter.com/validator)
3. **Preview on LinkedIn**: Use [LinkedIn Post Inspector](https://www.linkedin.com/post-inspector/)
4. **Check mobile view**: Ensure it looks good on small screens
5. **Verify file size**: Should load quickly

### After Publishing
1. Share your URL on social media to see how it appears
2. Check if image loads correctly
3. Verify text is readable
4. Ensure branding is clear
